Project Overview

Students will research a field of their choice and explore how programming is applied in that industry. They will then create a program that solves a real-world problem in that field, using the concepts learned in Structured Programming 1 and the design tools from Computer Science 1. The project will culminate in a presentation demonstrating their research and program.

Project Overview

Your task is to design, build, and program a functional device using an Arduino board that serves a purpose. The device should solve a real-world problem or make life more convenient. You will use the design cycle to guide your process, ensuring you research, plan, create, test, and refine your solution.
